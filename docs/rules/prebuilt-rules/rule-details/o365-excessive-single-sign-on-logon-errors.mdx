<div id="o365-excessive-single-sign-on-logon-errors"></div>

Identifies accounts with a high number of single sign-on (SSO) logon errors. Excessive logon errors may indicate an attempt to brute force a password or SSO token.

**Rule type**: threshold

**Rule indices**: 

* filebeat-*
* logs-o365*

**Severity**: high

**Risk score**: 73

**Runs every**: 5m

**Searches indices from**: now-20m ([Date Math format](((ref))/common-options.html#date-math), see also <DocLink id="enSecurityRulesUiCreate" section="set-the-rules-schedule">`Additional look-back time`</DocLink>)
