<div id="suspicious-hidden-child-process-of-launchd"></div>

Identifies the execution of a launchd child process with a hidden file. An adversary can establish persistence by installing a new logon item, launch agent, or daemon that executes upon login.

**Rule type**: query

**Rule indices**: 

* auditbeat-*
* logs-endpoint.events.*

**Severity**: medium

**Risk score**: 47

**Runs every**: 5m

**Searches indices from**: now-9m ([Date Math format](((ref))/common-options.html#date-math), see also <DocLink id="serverlessSecurityRulesUiCreate" section="set-the-rules-schedule">`Additional look-back time`</DocLink>)

## Rule query

```js
event.category:process and host.os.type:macos and event.type:(start or process_started) and
 process.name:.* and process.parent.executable:/sbin/launchd

```
