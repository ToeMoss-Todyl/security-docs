<div id="kerberos-cached-credentials-dumping"></div>

Identifies the use of the Kerberos credential cache (kcc) utility to dump locally cached Kerberos tickets. Adversaries may attempt to dump credential material in the form of tickets that can be leveraged for lateral movement.

**Rule type**: query

**Rule indices**: 

* auditbeat-*
* logs-endpoint.events.*

**Severity**: high

**Risk score**: 73

**Runs every**: 5m

**Searches indices from**: now-9m ([Date Math format](((ref))/common-options.html#date-math), see also <DocLink id="serverlessSecurityRulesUiCreate" section="set-the-rules-schedule">`Additional look-back time`</DocLink>)
