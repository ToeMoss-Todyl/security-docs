<div id="microsoft-365-teams-guest-access-enabled"></div>

Identifies when guest access is enabled in Microsoft Teams. Guest access in Teams allows people outside the organization to access teams and channels. An adversary may enable guest access to maintain persistence in an environment.

**Rule type**: query

**Rule indices**: 

* filebeat-*
* logs-o365*

**Severity**: medium

**Risk score**: 47

**Runs every**: 5m

**Searches indices from**: now-30m ([Date Math format](((ref))/common-options.html#date-math), see also <DocLink id="serverlessSecurityRulesUiCreate" section="set-the-rules-schedule">`Additional look-back time`</DocLink>)

## Rule query

```js
event.dataset:o365.audit and event.provider:(SkypeForBusiness or MicrosoftTeams) and
```
